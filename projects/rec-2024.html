The 2024 Ride Engineering Competition

The Ride Engineering Competition provides students with a hands-on opportunity to apply their engineering knowledge and skills in a real-world context. It also fosters collaboration, creativity, and innovation among participants, while offering valuable networking opportunities with industry professionals in the amusement park and entertainment industries.

Our submission was a Spinning Rollercoaster titled Pixie Party. The theme consisted of a "Fairy Rave" hosted deep in a magical forest. This idea stemed from two original ideas we had: an 80s disco party, and magical fairies. We couldn't decide which to do, so we combined them! I pitched the idea of a spinning ride vehicle as a way to add some additional excitement to the experience, and thus Pixie Party was born!
